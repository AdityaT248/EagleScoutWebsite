About Zero Waste Cutlery

Our mission is to make sustainable dining accessible for everyone

Our Story

Zero Waste Cutlery Rental was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to reduce the environmental impact of single-use cutlery while providing a premium dining experience for our customers.

What began as a small collection of high-quality cutlery has grown into a comprehensive rental service used by restaurants, event planners, and eco-conscious individuals across the country.

We believe that sustainability shouldn't mean compromising on quality or elegance. Our carefully curated collection of premium stainless steel cutlery proves that eco-friendly choices can elevate any dining experience.

Our Mission

At Zero Waste Cutlery, we're committed to making a measurable impact on plastic waste reduction. Each year, billions of plastic utensils end up in landfills and oceans, taking hundreds of years to decompose.

By providing a convenient, high-quality alternative to disposable cutlery, we're helping our customers significantly reduce their environmental footprint while still enjoying beautiful table settings.

We're proud to report that in 2023 alone, our rental service helped prevent over 50,000 pieces of disposable cutlery from entering landfills.

How We Do It

Our sustainable process from start to finish

1. Eco-Sourcing

We carefully source high-quality, durable cutlery designed to last for years.

2. Efficient Delivery

We optimize delivery routes to minimize carbon emissions during transportation.

3. Eco-Cleaning

We use biodegradable, non-toxic cleaning products in our sanitization process.

4. Complete Lifecycle

When cutlery reaches the end of its rental life, we recycle it responsibly.
